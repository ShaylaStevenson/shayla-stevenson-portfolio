About Me

I am a Full-stack Web Developer with experience in HTML5, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JQuery, APIs, Express.js, ORM, Node.js, MySQL, Sequelize, MVC structuring and NoSQL. The skills that I am acquiring through my education at the Trilogy Full-stack Web Development program allow me to develop fully functioning, responsive web applications. I also have experience in refactoring to improve the performance of existing websites.

My background in business management provides me a unique perspective in web development. I know the frustration that comes from using a clunky web application. At the heart of my designs is the concept of the user's experience. Your employees and customers will use my sites intuitivly, which is achieved by efficient flow and navigation throughout the applications.

Your website is your organization's home. As such, I want its guests to feel welcome and comfortable. Your website should represent your businesses' unique message and style. The first stage in my design process is to get to know your brand in order to create a space that supports its mission. Then, through frequent colaboration, we can build the site that fits you.
